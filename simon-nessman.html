Early Life

Simon Nessman was born on November 6, 1989, in Courtenay, British Columbia, Canada, the youngest of four children of Carolyn Ireland and Ron Nessman, and is of Austrian and Scottish descent. He attended Georges P. Vanier Secondary School, where he played basketball and rugby and received university offers on the strength of his athletics, from both the University of the Fraser Valley and Quest University in British Columbia.

Discovery and Modeling Career

Nessman was discovered in December 2006, during his final year of high school, after a friend of his sister sent his photos to talent scout Kelly Streit of Mode Models in Calgary. He did not begin modeling professionally until after graduating, then moved to New York with Major Management, where he was based for the next several years. His runway debut came in the Fall/Winter 2008 season, and the casting that established him as a high-fashion name soon followed, when Riccardo Tisci booked him for Givenchy across 2008 and 2009.

Through 2010 and 2011, Nessman modeled for Armani Jeans and the Emporio Armani Fall/Winter 2011 campaign, photographed by Mario Sorrenti. In 2012, Giorgio Armani signed him to a multi-year contract as the face of the Acqua di Giò Essenza fragrance, in a campaign photographed and directed by Bruce Weber that placed his image on billboards worldwide; he continued fronting the fragrance's print and video campaigns through 2014. From late 2010 to 2014 he also starred opposite Karmen Pedaru in multiple print and film campaigns for Michael Kors, shot by Mario Testino, and by 2017 he was the face of Polo Ralph Lauren under photographer Josh Olins. His other advertising work has included Versace, Dolce & Gabbana, Calvin Klein, Lanvin, Yves Saint Laurent, Barneys, Gap, and John Galliano, and he appeared alongside Cara Delevingne in John Hardy's Spring/Summer 2015 campaign, shot in Bali by Sebastian Faena. In 2012, Nessman appeared in Madonna's music video for "Girl Gone Wild," directed by Mert Alas and Marcus Piggott. His career has continued into the mid-2020s with campaigns for Calvin Klein, J.Crew, Pepe Jeans, Tommy Hilfiger, Mackage, and Todd Snyder.

In 2013, Nessman returned to Canada to complete an undergraduate degree at Quest University in Squamish, where he also played basketball, graduating in 2017 with a Bachelor of Arts and Science while continuing to model.

The Commercial Male — The Consensus

Nessman appears at No. 38 in "The Consensus," thecommercialmale.com's composite ranking across eight male-model ranking systems. He is measured by four of the eight: the Male Model Index (31), MaleIconic (31), and both of UOMO Modello's internal rankings (by-rank, No. 29, and power rankings, No. 27) — the latter two independent of the Male Model Index/MaleIconic pair. He does not appear on the original 1972–2012 L'Uomo Vogue list.

Industry Honors

In 2011, Nessman was ranked the second most successful male model in the world, behind Sean O'Pry, on Models.com's Top 50 list. In 2013, Forbes ranked him third on its World's Highest-Paid Male Models list, behind David Gandy and Sean O'Pry. He received Models.com Model of the Year nominations in 2014 and 2019, and Social Movers nominations in 2020 and 2023. As of 2015 he was featured on Models.com's Sexiest Men, Top Icons Men, and The Money Guys lists, and in 2019 he reached Supers Men, the site's highest classification for a male model.

Personal Life

Nessman lives on Vargas Island near Tofino, British Columbia, where surfing has remained a lifelong passion; he has scaled back his modeling schedule by choice while continuing to work regularly. In July 2020, he married Romanian model Andreea Diaconu.
