Early Life

Sean Richard O'Pry was born on July 5, 1989, in Kennesaw, Georgia, to a family of Irish and Native American heritage. He has an elder brother, Chris, and a younger sister, Shannon. He attended North Cobb High School, where he played track and field, basketball, baseball, and football, and has said he worked as a plumber for a period before modeling.

Discovery and Modeling Career

In 2006, at age 17, O'Pry was scouted after Nole Marin of AIM Model Management came across his prom photos on MySpace. He went on to model for Calvin Klein, Giorgio Armani, Versace, Dolce & Gabbana, Ralph Lauren, Gianfranco Ferré, H&M, Massimo Dutti, Zara, Marc Jacobs, Emporio Armani, Lacoste, DSquared², American Eagle, Bottega Veneta, DKNY, Fendi, and Hugo Boss, among others, walking runways and appearing on campaigns and magazine covers worldwide.

O'Pry rose quickly in the industry's rankings: Forbes named him the world's eighth most successful male model in 2008, and the following year moved him to the number-one spot, a position he held again when Forbes named him the highest-paid male model in 2013. Models.com ranked him the top male model on its "Top 50" list starting in September 2013, a position he held for two years, and in 2015 placed him on its inaugural "Supers Men," "Sexiest Men," and "Money Guys" lists. That same year, Vogue ranked him fifth on its list of the Top 10 Male Models of All Time.

Music Videos and Television

O'Pry appeared in Madonna's 2012 music video teaser for "Girl Gone Wild" and, most notably, starred opposite Taylor Swift in her 2014 music video for "Blank Space," a role that introduced him to a much wider mainstream audience. He also appeared in a 2016 episode of HBO's Veep.

In 2024, O'Pry pushed back publicly on his renewed online popularity within "looksmaxxing" communities, saying he has never considered himself superior to anyone because of his looks.

Personal Life

O'Pry lives in New York City. He has described himself as a homebody who enjoys spending time with family and visiting Waffle House rather than a lavish lifestyle, and has kept a pet dog.
