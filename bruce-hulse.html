Early Life

Bruce Hulse was born on March 13, 1960, in Haverford, Pennsylvania, and raised in nearby Narberth with three sisters. A straight-A student and standout athlete in basketball and track, he attended Cornell University, where he studied Buddhist and Hindu studies (variously described as an Asian studies major), and later took graduate-level coursework in clinical psychology at West Chester University.

After his freshman year at Cornell, Hulse tried out for the Avalon Beach Patrol in New Jersey, following friends who were already lifeguarding there. He spent roughly a decade as an Avalon lifeguard, and also played professional basketball in Sweden, before modeling redirected his career.

Modeling Career

Hulse's modeling career, which has now spanned more than four decades, took off after his years on the beach patrol. Taken under the wing of photographer Bruce Weber early on, he became one of the most recognizable faces of the 1980s and 1990s, known for iconic Calvin Klein and Levi's advertising and multiple GQ covers.

He worked with photographers including Herb Ritts, Bruce Weber, and Peter Lindbergh, and appeared alongside models such as Cindy Crawford, Elle Macpherson, Andie MacDowell, and Paulina Porizkova on runways in Paris and shoots in locations including Barbados. His editorial work has continued into recent decades, including shoots for British Vogue, Vogue Hommes, American Vogue, and British GQ Style.

Memoir and Other Ventures

Hulse is the author of a memoir, published as Sex, Love, and Fashion: A Memoir of a Male Model and later reissued as Love & Fashion: A Male Model's Journey, which recounts his career alongside figures including Calvin Klein, Janice Dickinson, Herb Ritts, Elle Macpherson, Andie MacDowell, Paulina Porizkova, and Cindy Crawford, as well as the losses the industry suffered during the AIDS crisis of the 1980s and 1990s. The updated edition adds an epilogue describing a near-death experience later in his life.

He served as a judge on the Bravo reality competition Manhunt: The Search for America's Most Gorgeous Male Model, and continues to work as a photographer and surfer.

Personal Life

Hulse lives in Southern California with his wife and two children.
