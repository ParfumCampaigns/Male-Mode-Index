Early Life

Marlon Luiz Teixeira was born on September 16, 1991, in Brazil, of Portuguese, Amerindian, and Japanese descent. He was introduced to the modeling industry by his grandmother, a family friend of Anderson Baumgartner, owner of Way Model Management in São Paulo, which became his mother agency.

Discovery and Modeling Career

Teixeira made his runway debut in 2008–2009 as an exclusive for Dior Homme's Spring/Summer 2009 show in Paris, a breakthrough moment that included shooting the season's campaign under Karl Lagerfeld's creative direction. Over the following years he became a fixture of European luxury runways, walking for Chanel, Balmain, Giorgio Armani, Emporio Armani, Dolce & Gabbana, DSquared², Roberto Cavalli, Jean Paul Gaultier, Philipp Plein, and Trussardi, and opening shows for Emporio Armani and Dior Homme.

In 2010, Teixeira became the face of Armani Exchange. In 2011, he was named the face of Diesel's "Fuel for Life Denim Collection" fragrance alongside Dutch model Marloes Horst, in a campaign shot by Terry Richardson with a video directed by Melina Matsoukas; he had earlier fronted Diesel's underwear line and an American Eagle holiday campaign. His other advertising work has included Custo Barcelona, Sergio K, Ermanno Scervino, Antony Morato, Iceberg, Pull & Bear, Ellus, and, in 2024, Colcci. He has appeared on the covers of L'Officiel Hommes, Essential Homme, Fiasco, Made in Brazil, Client, Hercules, Vogue Hommes, and Mister, and in editorials for GQ, Vogue España, Vogue Italia, V, Elle, W (with Ginnifer Goodwin), and Vanity Fair (with Shakira). His career has continued into the mid-2020s with campaigns including Dsquared²'s 2026 beachwear line and a 2026 L'Officiel Monaco cover.

The Commercial Male — The Consensus

Teixeira appears at No. 39 in "The Consensus," thecommercialmale.com's composite ranking across eight male-model ranking systems. He is measured by four of the eight: the Male Model Index (32), MaleIconic (32), and both of UOMO Modello's internal rankings (by-rank, No. 30, and power rankings, No. 35) — the latter two independent of the Male Model Index/MaleIconic pair. He does not appear on the original 1972–2012 L'Uomo Vogue list.

Industry Honors

Teixeira won Top Male Model at the theFashionSpot Style Awards in 2013, and was named best male model of 2011 by Client magazine, Made in Brazil, and Hola! magazine. He was formerly ranked in Models.com's Top 50, and has since appeared on its Trending Men, Top Icons Men, Sexiest Men, and Next Generation lists.

Personal Life

Teixeira has a tattoo of his mother Claudia's name near his waist. As of the mid-2020s he splits his time between Brazil and Bali, and is raising a daughter.
