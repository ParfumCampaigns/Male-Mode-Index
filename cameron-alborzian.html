Early Life

Cameron Alborzian was born on February 26, 1967, in Tehran, Iran, to an English mother and an Iranian father. He was sent to boarding school in England around the time of the 1979 Iranian Revolution, and was later scouted as a model while enrolled in a sports college in London.

Modeling Career

Alborzian's career launched in 1986 with an early runway appearance for Jean Paul Gaultier in Paris. He went on to become one of the first male supermodels of the era, working with Guess, Levi's, Versace, Dior, Valentino, Chanel, Gucci, Jean Paul Gaultier, Dolce & Gabbana, Karl Lagerfeld, John Galliano, Yves Saint Laurent, Vivienne Westwood, Louis Vuitton, Tom Ford, Ralph Lauren, Giorgio Armani, and Bijan, among others. His three-year Guess Jeans contract, shot in part by Ellen von Unwerth, helped define his 1980s image, and he was later photographed by Bruce Weber and Herb Ritts, including a 1990 Versace campaign with Christy Turlington shot by Ritts.

In 1989, Alborzian appeared as Madonna's love interest in her music video for "Express Yourself," directed by David Fincher, one of his most widely recognized media appearances. He was named Model of the Year in 1990 and again in 1994, and appeared regularly in Vogue and Elle alongside Naomi Campbell, Christy Turlington, and Kate Moss.

Transition to Yoga and Ayurveda

Alborzian left the fashion industry in the late 1990s, citing a 1998 meeting with Nelson Mandela during a South African fashion shoot, alongside the death of a close friend and mentor in naturopathy, as turning points. He trained at the Integral Yoga Institute and Yogaville in New York under Sri Swami Satchidananda, took a reflexology master's course at the Open Center in New York City, and continued his studies at the Arsha Yoga Vidya Peetam Trust in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India, under Sri Vasudevan.

Now working as Yogi Cameron, Alborzian has authored four books — The Guru in You, The Yogi Code, The One Plan, and Transform Your Health — and hosted the reality series Yogi Cameron: A Model Guru, which ran on Veria Living and ZLiving from 2012. He has appeared on The Dr. Oz Show, The Ellen DeGeneres Show, and Today to discuss Ayurveda and yoga, and serves as a goodwill ambassador for the Indian state of Kerala.

Personal Life

Alborzian is fluent in English, Farsi, and Spanish and lives in Los Angeles.

Editorial Note

Cameron Alborzian's career continues to be documented through independent editorial archives dedicated to preserving the history of commercial male modeling.
